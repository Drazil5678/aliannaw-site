About

Welcome to the world of Alianna – a passionate singer, actress, and performer. Originally from Maine and now based in Tampa Bay and New York City, Alianna is studying Musical Theater at Circle in the Square Theatre School on Broadway, on track to earn her BFA in 2026.

Her performance journey includes Shakespeare, contemporary political theatre, and musical theatre, bringing characters to life with heart, depth, and vocal strength. Known for professionalism, leadership, and emotional authenticity.
