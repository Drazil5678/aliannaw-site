About

Welcome to the world of Alianna – a passionate singer, actress, and performer. Originally from Maine and now based in Tampa Bay and New York City, she is studying Musical Theater at Circle in the Square Theatre School, earning her BFA in 2026.

Her work spans Shakespeare, political theater, and musical theatre, with a reputation for depth, professionalism, and strong vocal leadership.
